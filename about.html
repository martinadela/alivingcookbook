🍲🍲🍲 ABOUT 🍲🍲🍲 Cooking as an excercise of ~resilience~ and *resistance* in pandemic times.

In his book The Poetics of Space, Gaston Bachelard argues that our homes are poetic spaces that become alive through the way we inhabit them. They are indeed a reflection of ourselves. Our kitchens are living laboratories, and one of the only places in our homes where we can create and experiment. 🍉 🍊 🍅 🍍🍋 🍒 🍈🍆 🍐 🌽🍓 and many more fruits, vegetables and spices bring growth, energy, and transformation into our intimate spaces and invite us to act upon the present. Who knew, the unannounced arrival of a global virus would displace every possible future. Maybe for now, we can focus on the inside. How can art exist purely as a process? How can artistic practice adapt to a socially distanced world? If an essential part of cooking, is sharing, how can we do this from the confinement of home? 🌆🌇 Artists' Recipes in Confinement: A Living Cookbook is a project created by artist, writer and cook 🍴 Martina Miño Pérez 🍴 This platform is developed as part of the Alone-Together Artist Home Residency, organized and funded by Kone Foundation. This living kitchen is meant to collect through time, recipes from artists, writers, researchers, creatives, who have found refuge in the process of cooking in this current context and wish to express through food.🍧 This living archive will be updated regularly and is a space for sharing different processes, reflections, memories and stories related to food. Every week three new recipes are added to this living kitchen and will be available to be discovered. 🍕 We can digest our present circumstances collectively, from the confinement of our homes 🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠🏠 If you wish to know how to submit to this cookbook or just want to chat, you can write an e-mail to: 📝 alivingcookbook@gmail.com 📝 You can also follow @alivingcookbook on instagram to find out when new recipes are uploaded.🍳 Web Design/Software: Martina Miño Pérez/Sébastien Piquemal ✨✨Thank You✨✨ Kone Foundation for your valuable support.
